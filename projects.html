My Projects

WeCare: We care for our community.

This app is to help the community by providing interfaces for both help seekers and Volunteers. In this sophisticated world, everyone is busy with the work and no one gets time to look after the people and the community. This app will help the people in the community to get help from the volunteers. The app is mainly for help seekers and volunteers. The help seeker will open a ticket mentioning the requirement and the volunteer will accept the ticket if they are available at that time.

Role: we vertically sliced the functionalities and created user stories. I involved in creating mockups, user stories, designing actual screens and implementing functionalities such as Firebase authentication and xml designs.

Color Scheme: #F97677 #DCDCDC

Technologies Used: Anderoid(Java), Firebase

Font family: Comic Sans MS, Comic Sans, cursive

RoadSigns: We make exams easy!.

Post pandemic most of the classes and exams are conducted online. Due to prevailing situation, test takers across the world face difficulties in finding study materials or attend in-person classes. Following to that most of the businesses and application developers started implementing new applications to support education system. Since most of the application developers or business focus on making money, my aim is to help road test exam takers by making this application free and the profit can be gained by charging institutions or teachers based on the services used by them.

Role: Full application is developed by me. I designed the user interfaces, designed the database, implemented the logic and tested the end to end flow of the application.

Color Scheme: #6C7EBC #AF60BE

Technologies Used: Swift, Realm

Font family: SF Pro

BMI Calculator: Calculate your BMI easily, be healthy!

We all struggle at some point to find a good tool to measure our BMI. One of my friend struggled to find a good source to measure his BMI. I believed that their can be so many people who struggle to calculate their BMI. Also I noticed some people use charts and manually calculate their BMI. We should check our BMI once every 6 months and based on the result, we have to fix or improve our food habits. This app is for people who struggles to measure their BMI.
